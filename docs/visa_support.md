# Visa Support Reference

## Transaction Issues
Visa cardholders may report transaction declines, card verification issues, or merchant acceptance questions.
For a declined transaction, cardholders should confirm card details, available balance, and merchant category acceptance.
Pending transaction timelines depend on issuer processing windows and merchant settlement behavior, typically 1-3 business days.

## Card Verification
If a card is not accepted at a merchant, the cardholder should verify the card is activated and the billing address matches.
Contactless payment issues can often be resolved by inserting the card instead or restarting the terminal.

## Fraud and Security (Escalate Always)
Fraud alerts, unauthorized charges, stolen cards, and card security concerns require immediate escalation to secure support channels.
Do not attempt to resolve fraud reports through standard support — always escalate to the fraud team immediately.

## Account Management (Escalate Always)
PIN reset, account ownership verification, and restricted access to card controls require manual identity verification.
Cardholders who suspect their card number has been compromised should request a card replacement through the issuer immediately.
